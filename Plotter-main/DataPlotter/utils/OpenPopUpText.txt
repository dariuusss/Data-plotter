Welcome to our new app, Plotit!

Plotit is a lightweight app that allows you to visualize your data on a console.
You can use it to plot your data in a simple and fast way. We currently support scatter plots, which are a fun way to visualize your data!
Find the best fit for your data, using our regression tools (which can automatically detect the optimal regression) or predict the future values of your data using our forecasting tools!
Or maybe you're a bit more adventurous and would like to calculate the integral of your data? We've got you covered!

Thank you for choosing us, we hope you'll enjoy our app!
